Chapter 1 — What Is BanteeX?

BanteeX is a multi-confluence institutional trading indicator built on Smart Money Concepts (SMC) combined with ICT Kill Zone timing. It tracks what institutional traders—banks, hedge funds, and market makers—do on the chart, and alerts you to enter after they have shown their hand, not before.

Core Philosophy: Institutions leave 3 clues: Liquidity Sweeps, Order Blocks, and Fair Value Gaps. BanteeX detects all 3 simultaneously.
