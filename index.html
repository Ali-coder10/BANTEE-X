Section 01
🧠 What Is This Indicator?
CONCEPT OVERVIEW

This is a multi-confluence institutional trading indicator built on Smart Money Concepts (SMC) combined with ICT Kill Zone timing. It tracks what institutional traders (banks, hedge funds, market makers) do on the chart and alerts you to enter after they've shown their hand — not before. You are reacting to institutional footprints, not predicting price.

CORE PHILOSOPHY

Institutions cannot hide their movements. They leave 3 clues on every chart: Liquidity sweeps (stop hunts), Order blocks (zones of interest), and Fair Value Gaps (imbalances they need to fill). This indicator detects all 3 simultaneously — and only fires a signal when they align inside a Kill Zone window.

Section 03
📊 What Each Visual Element Means
💧
SWEEP (Sellside)
Price wicked below recent swing low and closed back above it. Stop losses of short-holders were hunted. Institutions bought. Look for a long setup.
💧
SWEEP (Buyside)
Price wicked above recent swing high and closed back below. Long stop losses were triggered. Institutions sold. Look for a short setup.
📦
Bull OB (Cyan Box)
Last bearish candle before a strong bullish impulse. This is where institutions placed buy orders. Price respects this zone on retest.
📦
Bear OB (Red Box)
Last bullish candle before a strong bearish impulse. Institutional sell zone. Price rejects from here when retested from below.
▬
Bull FVG (Green Box)
3-candle imbalance where buyers moved so fast they left a gap. Acts as a magnet — price often returns to fill it. Entry zone for longs.
▬
Bear FVG (Orange Box)
Sellers created an imbalance gap on the downside. Price pulls back into this zone before continuing down. Entry zone for shorts.
🏗️
BOS ▲ / BOS ▼
Break of Structure. Price confirmed a new swing high (bullish BOS) or swing low (bearish BOS). This sets and confirms the directional bias.
🔄
CHoCH ▲ / CHoCH ▼
Change of Character. Structure shifted against the prevailing trend — the most important confirmation signal for a reversal trade entry.

Section 06
🎯 Step-by-Step Trade Execution
1
CHECK DASHBOARD BEFORE LONDON / NY OPENS

Open TradingView 5 minutes before Kill Zone starts. Check the dashboard: What is HTF Bias? Where is Asian High/Low? Is there an open FVG or OB nearby? Build your mental map before price starts moving.

2
WAIT FOR THE 💧 SWEEP LABEL

Do nothing until a Sweep label appears on the chart. If it says "SWEEP (Sellside)" — institutions hunted longs below, reversal up expected. If it says "SWEEP (Buyside)" — institutions hunted stops above, reversal down expected. This is your trigger event.

3
CONFIRM THE SIGNAL GRADE IN DASHBOARD

Immediately look at the Dashboard → Signal Grade field. If it says ⭐ A-GRADE — proceed to entry. If it says B-Grade, reduce size or skip. If it says Waiting — sit on your hands. Grade determines sizing, not emotion.

4
FIND THE ORDER BLOCK ON 15M CHART

Look for the cyan (Bull OB) or red (Bear OB) box near the sweep zone. Wait for price to pull back INTO this box. The 50% level of the OB box is your ideal limit order entry. If OB has an FVG inside it — that's your exact entry zone.

5
WAIT FOR CHoCH CONFIRMATION

The CHoCH label (Change of Character) must appear on the 5M or 15M chart after price taps the OB. A CHoCH ▲ = buyer confirmation for longs. A CHoCH ▼ = seller confirmation for shorts. No CHoCH = no entry. This single rule eliminates most false signals.

6
EXECUTE WITH PROPER SL & 3 TPs

Enter at the OB 50% level or on CHoCH candle close. Place SL beyond the sweep wick. Use 3 targets below:

Section 07
🛡️ Non-Negotiable Risk Rules
  • Max risk per trade: 1–2% of total account only
  • Minimum Risk-to-Reward before entry: 2:1 minimum — if you can't get 2R, skip the trade
  • Max trades per Kill Zone session: 2 trades
  • Max trades per full day: 3 trades total
  • If 2 stop losses hit in same day: Stop trading, close platform, come back tomorrow
  • Always check economic calendar (CoinMarketCap / Forex Factory) before London and NY opens — news events override all technical setups
  • Never move SL further from entry to avoid being stopped out — that is the most dangerous habit in trading
  • After TP1 is hit, always move SL to breakeven. A free trade running = no stress trading

Section 09
🚫 When NOT to Trade
  • Dashboard shows ⚫ No KZ — you are outside all Kill Zone windows
  • Dashboard shows — Waiting in Signal Grade — no setup has formed
  • A major news event (Fed speech, CPI, major crypto news) drops within 30 minutes of your potential entry
  • HTF Bias is unclear or ranging — no clear BOS in either direction on 4H chart
  • Sweep wick was too deep (more than 2–3%) — this may be a genuine breakout, not a sweep
  • You have already hit 2 stop losses today — the market is not in your favor today, walk away
